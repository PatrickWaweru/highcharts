You see agroclimatic data based on the NorESM1-M model provided by the Norwegian Climate Centre on behalf of the Copernicus Climate Change Service. The data covers the time span from 1951 to 2010 as 10-day average with a temporal resolution of 10 years. The Copernicus Observation Programme uses historical data from ground and satellites. More information can be found at the Climate Data Store of the European Union.
